What to include

A clear email makes it much easier to help you quickly.

  • Your iPhone model if the issue is device related.
  • What you were trying to do in TapLock.
  • What happened instead.
  • Whether the issue is with NFC, app blocking, or setup.
  • A screenshot or screen recording if it helps explain the problem.

FAQ

Before you email.

These answers may help if you are trying to understand how TapLock works.

Do I need an NFC card?

Yes. TapLock is designed around using an NFC card to start and stop focus sessions.

Can I ask for help with setup?

Yes. Send an email explaining what part of setup is confusing or not working.

Should I include screenshots?

Screenshots are helpful if you are seeing an error, a confusing screen, or something that does not look right.

Can I send feedback?

Yes. Feedback is welcome, especially if there is something that would make TapLock easier to use.
