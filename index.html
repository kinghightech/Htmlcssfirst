How it works

A simple focus system that does not need to be complicated.

TapLock is built around one idea: starting a focus session should be quick. Instead of opening a bunch of settings every time, you use an NFC card to turn your block on or off.

Questions

Quick answers.

Here are the basic things people usually want to know before using TapLock.

Do I need an NFC card?

Yes. TapLock is designed around using an NFC card so the lock feels more physical and less like another easy button inside your phone.

What apps can I block?

You choose the distracting apps you want to block. The point is to make your own focus setup based on what actually wastes your time.

Is TapLock hard to set up?

No. The website and app are meant to be simple. Pick your apps, connect your card, and use the tap when you want to lock in.

Who is TapLock made for?

TapLock is made for students, creators, coders, builders, and anyone who wants a cleaner way to stay away from distracting apps.
